About Our School

Our Mission

Alok Central School is dedicated to providing a comprehensive education programme for its students, emphasising the developmental needs of the child as a whole.

Our Vision

Alok Central School aims at the all-round development of children into well-disciplined, academically oriented, and self-confident individuals who will go on to make a mark in global society and achieve excellence in all walks of life. We endeavour to do this by providing a comprehensive curriculum that enables students to deal with the present and prepare for the future in a stress-free but stimulating environment.

About Us

Alok Central School, Shahpura, was established in 2002 and is managed privately. Located in the rural area of Shahpura block, Bhilwara district, Rajasthan, the school serves Grades 1 to 10 as a co-educational institution with an attached pre-primary section.

Hindi is the medium of instruction, and the academic session starts in April. The school has a private building with 11 classrooms in good condition, 2 rooms for non-teaching activities, and a separate room for the Head Master/Teacher.

Facilities include electric connection, tap water, 2 boys' and 2 girls' toilets (all functional), a playground, a library with 200 books, 6 functional computers, a computer-aided learning lab, and mid-day meal provision prepared on school premises.
