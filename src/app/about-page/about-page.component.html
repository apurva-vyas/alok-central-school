About Alok Central School, Shahpura

Our Mission

Alok Central School, Shahpura, is dedicated to providing a comprehensive education programme for its students in Shahpura and surrounding areas of Bhilwara district, Rajasthan — emphasising the developmental needs of the child as a whole.

Our Vision

Alok Central School aims at the all-round development of children into well-disciplined, academically oriented, and self-confident individuals who will go on to make a mark in global society and achieve excellence in all walks of life. As the leading school in Shahpura, Bhilwara, we endeavour to do this by providing a comprehensive curriculum that enables students to deal with the present and prepare for the future in a stress-free but stimulating environment.

About Alok Central School

Alok Central School, Shahpura, was established in 2002 and is managed privately. Located in Shahpura block, Bhilwara district, Rajasthan, the school serves Grades 1 to 10 as a co-educational institution with an attached pre-primary section. We are recognized as one of the best schools in Shahpura for our commitment to quality education and holistic development.

Hindi is the medium of instruction, and the academic session starts in April. The school has a private building with 11 classrooms in good condition, 2 rooms for non-teaching activities, and a separate room for the Head Master/Teacher.

Facilities include electric connection, tap water, 2 boys' and 2 girls' toilets (all functional), a playground, a library with 200 books, 6 functional computers, a computer-aided learning lab, and mid-day meal provision prepared on school premises. Parents across Shahpura and Bhilwara district trust us for quality education and character development.
